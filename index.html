What It Does

Modern search has three layers. The Claude SEO Skill covers all of them — plus structured data, content quality, and a beautiful downloadable report.

SEO Audit

Deep technical and on-page analysis covering title tags, meta descriptions, heading hierarchy, canonicalization, internal linking, Core Web Vitals signals, and crawlability.

  • Title & meta optimization
  • Heading structure analysis
  • Internal link audit
  • Canonicalization check
  • Image alt text review
  • Page speed signals
GEO Audit

Generative Engine Optimization — how likely is your content to be cited, quoted, or recommended by AI systems like Claude, ChatGPT, and Perplexity?

  • Source credibility signals
  • Citation-worthy formatting
  • E-E-A-T assessment
  • Entity clarity check
  • AI-snippet readiness
  • Content authority signals
AEO Audit

Answer Engine Optimization — is your content structured to appear in featured snippets, voice search results, and AI-generated direct answers?

  • Question-answer structure
  • Featured snippet suitability
  • FAQ & HowTo schema
  • Conversational query match
  • Voice search optimization
  • Concise answer density
Structured Data Review

Validates existing schema markup and recommends missing JSON-LD schemas that unlock rich results — articles, products, events, FAQs, breadcrumbs, and more.

  • JSON-LD validation
  • Rich result eligibility
  • Missing schema detection
  • Schema.org alignment
Content Quality Analysis

Evaluates readability, topical depth, keyword use, content gaps, and semantic coverage to ensure your pages fully satisfy search intent.

  • Readability scoring
  • Topical coverage gaps
  • Keyword density review
  • Search intent alignment
Downloadable DOCX + PDF Report

Every audit auto-generates a premium agency-quality report — a polished Word document and PDF you can share with clients or your team.

  • Navy cover with score cards
  • Signal-by-signal findings table
  • Priority matrix (Critical → Quick Win)
  • Executive summary included
  • "What's Working Well" section

Search has evolved beyond the blue link. To win today, you need to optimize for all three layers.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Claude SEO Skill?
The Claude SEO Skill is a specialized capability you invoke inside any Claude conversation. It performs comprehensive SEO, GEO, and AEO audits for any website URL you provide. Simply invoke it and paste your URL; Claude handles crawling, scoring, and generating a professional downloadable report.
What is Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)?
GEO is the practice of optimizing your content so that AI-powered search engines — like ChatGPT Search, Perplexity, Claude, and Gemini — are more likely to cite, recommend, or reference your site when answering user queries. Unlike traditional SEO which targets algorithms, GEO targets AI retrieval systems and focuses on source credibility, well-structured factual content, clear entity signals, and authority indicators.
What is Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)?
AEO is optimizing your content to become the direct answer — not just a ranked result. This includes appearing in Google's AI Overviews, featured snippets, People Also Ask boxes, and voice search results. AEO focuses on structuring content with clear question-answer pairs, adding FAQ and HowTo schema markup, and writing concise, definitive answers that answer engines can directly surface.
How is this different from tools like Ahrefs or Semrush?
Ahrefs and Semrush are great for backlink research, keyword tracking, and competitive analysis — but they don't audit for GEO or AEO. The Claude SEO Skill goes beyond traditional SEO to analyze how your content performs in the AI-search era, and delivers an instant agency-quality DOCX + PDF report with zero subscription cost.
What's the difference between Quick and Full Audit?
The Quick Audit takes 1–2 minutes and covers the highest-priority signals across up to 7 key pages — ideal for a fast health check. The Full Audit takes 5–10 minutes and crawls every meaningful page on your site with no arbitrary cap, giving you the most comprehensive SEO, GEO, and AEO picture possible. Both produce a DOCX + PDF report.
Does it really crawl the whole site, not just the homepage?
Yes. Claude fetches your homepage, robots.txt, and sitemap.xml in parallel to build a complete map of your site. It then crawls your About, Services, Blog, Case Studies, Contact, and FAQ pages — drawing conclusions from the full picture, not just your front door.
Do I need a Claude subscription to use this?
The skill itself is completely free and open source. However, since it runs inside Claude, you'll need access to Claude — either through Claude.ai (free tier available) or via the Anthropic API. For best results with Full Audits on large sites, Claude Pro or API access is recommended due to the amount of content being processed.
